Milestone State Testing

Ensure seamless administration of tests at 60 sites, supporting 5,000 students and 500 educators without technology disruptions.

Deliverables

1. Network evaluation reports for each testing site.

2. Complete inventory and delivery completion of equipment.

3. Equipment testing and maintenance records.

4. Training documentation for team members.

5. Onsite support documentation and remote support plan.

6. Test completion reports showing 100% student participation without disruptions.

7. Lessons learned document emphasizing the role of preparation and stakeholder updates in project success.

Results

The Milestones Testing project successfully achieved its goals:

  • 100% of students completed their tests without technology disruptions.

  • The project remained within budget and did not incur unexpected costs.

  • All equipment was accounted for and remained functional throughout the testing period.

  • Generated six-figure revenue for the company and attracted interest from other educational institutions for similar testing initiatives.

Lessons Learned

Effective preparation and continuous updates with stakeholders were critical to the project's success. Clear communication and proactive planning ensured that potential issues were addressed before they impacted testing. Regular updates and training kept the team well-prepared to handle any challenges that arose, contributing to the seamless execution of the testing process.
