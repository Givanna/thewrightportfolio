About Me

Hi! I’m Givanna, a first-generation American whose work ethic and passion for perseverance come from my parents’ inspiring journey to build a new life. Their story drives my dedication to continuous growth, both personally and professionally.

One way I channel this passion is through project management, where I believe success requires a wide range of skills. My engineering background helps me understand complex technical challenges, while my experience in technical writing allows me to create clear communication and effective documentation. For me, each project is more than just a series of tasks; it’s about fostering strong relationships with everyone involved. I know that without those relationships, meeting project goals becomes a challenge, but when everything comes together, there’s no better feeling!

Volunteering is another essential part of my life. I believe in giving back to others in the same way I’ve been fortunate to receive support and mentorship throughout my life. Making a positive impact in my community isn’t just a goal; it’s a fundamental part of who I am.

Thank you for stopping by my website! I hope you enjoy exploring my projects and the stories behind them. Don’t forget to check back often as I’ll be adding more exciting work along the way!
